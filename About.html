Meet Suraj Kori: Bridging the Educational Divide through Innovation and Tinkering

Welcome to my website! Im Suraj Kori, a passionate Electronics and Communication Engineering student with a profound interest in electronics, technology, education, and social work.

Tinkering India Initiative: Fostering Creativity in Rural Education

Currently, I serve as a Mentor for the Tinkering India Initiative, where I play a pivotal role in guiding and supporting the lead team to foster innovation and creativity. My expertise lies in extending hands-on tinkering education to rural villages, empowering young minds, and nurturing their problem-solving skills. My responsibilities include mentoring, curriculum development, and community engagement, all aimed at narrowing the educational divide in underserved regions.

Empowering Through Practical Electronics Education

As an Electronics Educator at Tinkering India, I specialize in imparting practical electronics knowledge to students in rural settings. I design and conduct hands-on workshops that introduce young learners to the fundamental concepts and projects in electronics, sparking their interest in technology and innovation. My work spans mentoring, curriculum development, and community outreach, with a focus on inspiring future engineers and inventors.

Laboratory Coordination at Dr. Kalam Innovative School

In my role as the Laboratory Coordinator at Dr. Kalam Innovative School, I manage the STEM, Tinkering, and AI labs. I oversee lab activities, ensure the availability of resources, and maintain essential equipment. I am committed to facilitating enriching hands-on learning experiences, supporting both students and teachers in their projects, and promoting an environment conducive to innovation and creativity.

Project Management at Eduneuro

Additionally, I am a Project Management Intern at Eduneuro, where I assist in the planning, execution, and oversight of educational projects. My duties include task coordination, timeline management, and achieving project goals efficiently. I work closely with team members to optimize processes and improve the outcomes of our educational endeavors.

Designing STEM Modules for Sais Angel Foundation

As a STEM Module Designer at Sais Angel Foundation, I develop engaging and educational STEM curricula. I create interactive modules that demystify complex concepts, making them accessible and exciting for students. My collaboration with educators ensures that our content meets educational standards and motivates young minds to delve into the realms of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Through innovative design and hands-on activities, I am dedicated to cultivating a lasting passion for STEM learning.

Literary Pursuits

Beyond my technical and educational endeavors, I am also a poet and writer. My works have been featured in the Yuvalay magazines YuvaConnect Edition 11, and I maintain a blog at Suraj Kori’s Poerty where I post my Hindi poems. My literary contributions reflect my deep connection to cultural roots and a commitment to expressing the human experience through poetry.

Join me on this journey as we explore the possibilities and impact the future of education and innovation together

Hello,

I’m Suraj

An Electronics and Communication Engineering student with a deep passion for technology, education, and social work. As a mentor for the Tinkering India Initiative, I actively promote innovation in rural education through practical, hands-on tinkering experiences.

In addition to my mentoring role, I serve as an Electronics Educator and Laboratory Coordinator at Dr. Kalam Innovative School. Here, I manage STEM, Tinkering, and AI labs, ensuring that both students and teachers have the resources they need for innovative projects. Join me as I blend education with innovation and creativity, shaping the future of learning and technological exploration.
