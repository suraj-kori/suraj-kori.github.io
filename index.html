Hello,

I’m Suraj

An Electronics and Communication Engineering student with a deep passion for technology, education, and social work. As a mentor for the Tinkering India Initiative, I actively promote innovation in rural education through practical, hands-on tinkering experiences.

In addition to my mentoring role, I serve as an Electronics Educator and Laboratory Coordinator at Dr. Kalam Innovative School. Here, I manage STEM, Tinkering, and AI labs, ensuring that both students and teachers have the resources they need for innovative projects. I am also a Project Management Intern at Eduneuro, where I oversee the execution and management of educational projects.

Beyond my technical roles, I am a writer and poet, with works featured in Yuvalay’s YuvaConnect. I maintain a blog at Suraj Kori’s Poetry, where I share my Hindi poetry, connecting with readers through my creative expressions.

Join me as I blend education with innovation and creativity, shaping the future of learning and technological exploration.
